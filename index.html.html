Coffee Shop

Coffee is a popular beverage made from roasted coffee beans, which are the seeds of berries from the Coffea plant.
It is known for its rich aroma, bold flavor, and stimulating effect due to its caffeine content.
Originating in Ethiopia, coffee has become a global staple, enjoyed in various forms such as espresso, latte, cappuccino, and cold brew.
Besides itsenergizing qualities, coffee is also a cultural and social experience, often shared in cafés and gatherings.
